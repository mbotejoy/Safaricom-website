Safaricom Mpesa Services

Mpesa is a mode by which people across Africa are able to transfer money to each other from anywhere as long as there is an agent near by. This initiative over the years has become our core function.

To make life easier we came up with a Mpesa App , Here you can check your balance easily. We have Mpesa services which include being able to withdraw or deposit cash, Sending or receiving money and also paying for goods and services. Our network is secure and confidentiality is maintained
